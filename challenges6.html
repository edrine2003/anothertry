Challenges & Needs

Our Challenges

Running a nurturing and supportive home for vulnerable children comes with many challenges, including:

  • Limited funding to meet daily food, clothing, and medical needs.
  • Access to quality education and learning materials for all children.
  • Providing adequate healthcare and sanitation facilities.
  • Maintaining a safe and comfortable shelter environment.
  • Emotional and psychological support for children who have experienced trauma.

Our Needs

To continue providing care and support, Hope and Faith Center currently needs:

  • Financial donations to cover operational costs and emergencies.
  • Educational materials: books, stationery, uniforms, and digital learning tools.
  • Food supplies and nutritious meals for the children.
  • Clothing and hygiene products.
  • Volunteers and skilled professionals to support education and counseling.
  • Medical supplies and access to regular health check-ups.
