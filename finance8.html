Financial Accountability

We deeply value the trust of our donors. Every contribution is carefully managed and used to directly benefit the children. Here's how we stay accountable:

  • We maintain detailed records of all donations and expenses.
  • Monthly financial summaries are reviewed by our leadership team.
  • We share annual reports with donors upon request.
  • Receipts are provided for all major donations.
  • Funds are only used for child care, education, health, and basic needs—not for personal or unrelated purposes.

If you'd like to see how your donation is used, feel free to contact us. Transparency is our promise.
