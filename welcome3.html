Welcome Message

Welcome to the Hope and Faith Center Orphanage. We are honored to share our journey with you and thankful that you are taking the time to learn more about us. Our organization was founded in 2024 with a deep desire to care for, support, and empower orphaned and vulnerable children in Mityana, Uganda.

At Hope and Faith Center, we believe that every child deserves love, a sense of belonging, and the opportunity to thrive. Through the generosity of supporters like you, we are making that belief a reality every day.

About Us

Hope and Faith Center is a community-based orphanage that provides shelter, food, education, and emotional care to 24 children. We are located in the heart of Mityana District, Uganda, and were established in 2024 to respond to the growing needs of vulnerable children in our area.

Our mission is to nurture hope and build a foundation of faith in each child's life, equipping them with the values and resources needed for a brighter future. We work tirelessly to create a safe, loving environment where children can heal, grow, and flourish.

Our vision is a world where every orphaned child has access to basic needs, education, and a chance to achieve their dreams—regardless of their background or circumstances.
